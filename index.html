About us

Welcome to the Columbia Gorge Werewolf Watchers club, for those fascinated by the Pacific Northwest's most elusive nocturnal creature. Founded by a group of enthusiastic (and slightly eccentric) nature lovers, our club is dedicated to the serious study and occasional spotting of the mythical Columbia Gorge Werewolf. Join us as we explore the mysteries of the night with some night vision goggles and snacks.

Werewolf spotting tips

  1. Moonlit Vigils: Werewolves are notoriously shy, but they can't resist a good full moon. Plan your stakeouts on cloudless nights.
  2. Stealth is Key: Wear soft-soled shoes (Crocs work best) and avoid stepping on twigs. Werewolves have excellent hearing!
  3. Avoid Scented Lotions: ou want to observe werewolves, not attract every mosquito in the gorge.
  4. Bring Snacks: Not for the werewolves, but for you. Stakeouts can be long, and a hungry watcher is not a happy watcher.
